 K-zoo Skate Zoo is Michigan's Oldest Skateboard Park.  Skater Owned and Operated Since 1990

We are Located in Kalamazoo Michigan. The Skate Zoo allows Skateboarding, Inline skating and Bikes (bikes-weekdays only/w full membership). We have the best spine ramp and a great street course. 6800 sq. feet of skating area inside .The Skate Zoo added a huge outdoor street course in July of 2000. Another 7200 sq ft. of skating area. Entirely surfaced with Skatelite. It is a blast to ride. You have to see it to believe it.
