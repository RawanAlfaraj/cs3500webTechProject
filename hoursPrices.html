Who Can Ride At the Skate Zoo?
  • Riders Must be age 7 or Older
  • We Allow Skateboards, Inline Skates, Bikes
  • Bikers Must Have a Full Membership to Ride ($65)
  • Bikers Can Ride Weekdays Only
  • Bikers Must Have Brakes

  • No Exceptions To this Policy-Don't Ask!

  • No Scooters 

  • No Wax

What Safety Gear Do I need?

  • A Helmet Is Required If you are Under 18
  • Elbow and Knee Pads are recommended
  • We Rent Helmets For $2
